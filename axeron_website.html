Tekla Structures 3D Modelling

We build complete, fabrication-ready Tekla Structures 3D models for industrial skids, structural steel, and complex assemblies. All models are organized with proper hierarchy, naming conventions, and phase markings ready for drawing extraction and downstream use.

3D Model Deliverables

  • Complete Tekla Structures 3D model
  • Main frame, skid beams, base frame
  • Equipment supports and pipe support brackets
  • Stiffeners, gussets, and lifting lugs / padeyes
  • Gratings, handrails, and ladders
  • Anchor bolt layouts

GA (General Arrangement) Drawings

  • Skid overall GA (plan, elevation, sections)
  • Equipment layout integration (if provided)
  • Pipe support GA (if applicable)
  • Lifting arrangement GA
  • Transportation support GA
  • Bolt and weld details overview

Model Review & Coordination

  • IFC export for client reviewers
  • Trimble Connect model sharing
  • Revision-based model modifications
  • Comment resolution sheet
  • Clash check report (model-based)
  • Weight summary & CoG (centre of gravity) report

Fabrication & Shop Drawings

We produce a full suite of fabrication drawings directly from Tekla models — single part drawings, assembly drawings, weld maps, and marking plans — all aligned to project specifications and fabrication standards.

Fabrication Drawings

  • Single part drawings
  • Assembly drawings
  • Full weld maps
  • Marking plans for all assembly groups
  • Fabrication notes per project specifications
  • Camber details (if applicable)
  • Cutting, cope, and bevel details
  • Revision cloud updates

GA Drawings

  • Skid overall GA drawing (plan, elevation, sections)
  • Lifting arrangement GA
  • Transportation support GA
  • Equipment layout integration

Reports Included

  • Assembly/erection sequence report
  • Weld volume report
  • Weight summary

Connection Detailing

We provide thorough connection detailing for both bolted and welded configurations, covering shop welds, field welds, gussets, stiffeners, and lifting reinforcements with full reference tables.

Connection Details

  • Bolted and welded connection detailing
  • Shop weld details (size, type, length)
  • Field weld indication
  • Connection numbering and reference tables
  • Gusset, stiffener, and bracket detailing
  • Lifting lug reinforcement details

BOM & NC File Generation

We extract and deliver complete bills of materials and fabrication data files directly from the Tekla model — covering bolt lists, assembly lists, material summaries, and machine-ready NC output for plate cutting and CNC fabrication.

Bill of Materials (BOM)

  • Bolt lists
  • Assembly lists
  • Material lists
  • Single part lists
  • Delivered in Excel, PDF, or custom formats

Fabrication Data Files

  • DSTV / NC files for CNC machines
  • DXF files for plate cutting
  • DWG files for documentation

Reports

  • Clash check report (model-based)
  • Weld volume report
  • Weight summary and CoG report
  • Assembly and erection sequence report

CAD Piping Design

We provide 2D and 3D CAD piping design services for industrial plants and process skids — from initial layout drafting to pipe support design and final isometric drawing production.

Deliverables

  • 2D piping layout drawings (plan and elevation)
  • 3D piping layout models
  • Pipe support design and GA drawings
  • Isometric drawing generation
  • Pipe routing coordination with structural model

STAAD Pro Structural Analysis

We use STAAD Pro to perform structural analysis and design for steel structures — generating load calculations, member sizing, and compliance documentation to support fabrication and engineering sign-off.

Analysis Scope

  • Static and dynamic load analysis
  • Dead, live, wind, and seismic load combinations
  • Member sizing and section selection
  • Support and foundation reaction reports
  • Code compliance verification (IS, BS, AISC)

Deliverables

  • STAAD analysis report
  • Member design summary
  • Load combination output sheets
  • Displacement and deflection report
